Our Story

Crafting Heritage
Since 1985

Prajapati Store was born from generations of pottery tradition. What began as a small workshop in Rajasthan has grown into a beloved brand that brings the magic of handcrafted Indian pottery to homes across the country.

Every product is crafted by hand, with love. Our artisans use traditional techniques — hand-throwing on the wheel, hand-painting, and kiln-firing — ensuring each piece is truly unique. We're committed to sustainability: all our materials are natural, our packaging is eco-friendly, and our Ganesha idols are 100% safe for immersion.

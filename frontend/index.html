Our Story

Prajapati Store

At Prajapati Store, we bring the timeless beauty of handcrafted pottery to your home. Inspired by Vadodara’s pottery tradition, we offer authentic clay products made by skilled local artisans.

Each piece is crafted using hand throwing, hand printing, and kiln firing, making it truly unique. From elegant home décor and everyday pottery to 100% biodegradable Ganesha idols, our collection blends heritage, craftsmanship, and sustainability.

We support traditional artisans and promote eco-friendly living. Made from natural clay and sustainable materials, our products celebrate Indian craftsmanship while adding warmth, simplicity, and authenticity to every home.
